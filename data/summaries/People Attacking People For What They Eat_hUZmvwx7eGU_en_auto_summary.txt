In this episode of the Medical Medium podcast, the host discusses the ongoing 'food wars' between plant-based and animal protein advocates, exploring how both sides often attack each other's dietary choices. The episode highlights the confusion and judgment surrounding food choices, especially among those with chronic illnesses, and emphasizes the need for understanding rather than division. The host encourages listeners to check out various episodes of the podcast, which provide valuable information for improving health and well-being.
