In this episode of the Medical Medium radio show, Anthony William discusses the importance of addressing the root causes of skin, hair, and nail health issues, emphasizing the role of the liver and the impact of diet and stress. He debunks the myth of topical miracle cures and highlights the significance of nutrients like beta carotene and silica from fresh fruits and vegetables. William encourages listeners to focus on internal healing and offers insights into dietary and herbal remedies for improving overall health and wellness, particularly for those suffering from chronic illnesses.
