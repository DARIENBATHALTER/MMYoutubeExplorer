In this episode of the Medical Medium podcast, Anthony William discusses the hidden dangers of tattoo inks, emphasizing that all currently available tattoo inks contain toxic heavy metals. He explains how these metals can leach into the bloodstream over time, potentially leading to health issues. The episode also highlights the importance of being proactive about detoxifying these metals, especially for those who already have tattoos.
