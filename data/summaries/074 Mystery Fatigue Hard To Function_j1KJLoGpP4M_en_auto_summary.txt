In this episode of the Medical Medium podcast, Anthony William discusses the widespread issue of chronic fatigue, emphasizing that many people suffer in silence from various conditions, including anxiety and autoimmune diseases. He distinguishes between normal tiredness and debilitating fatigue that significantly affects daily life, attributing it largely to viral infections like Epstein-Barr virus. The podcast aims to provide insights into the underlying causes of this mystery fatigue and offers hope for recovery through proper understanding and treatment.
