In this episode of the Medical Medium podcast, Anthony William discusses the therapeutic benefits of fruit picking as a form of meditation that helps to heal anxiety, PTSD, and rejuvenate the spirit. He emphasizes the importance of connecting with nature and the act of picking fruit as a sacred practice that fosters gratitude towards the Earth. The meditation guides listeners through a visualization of fruit picking, aimed at reigniting past experiences and promoting emotional healing.
