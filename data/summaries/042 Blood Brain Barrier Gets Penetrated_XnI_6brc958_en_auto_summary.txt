In this episode of the Medical Medium podcast, Anthony Williams discusses the inadequacies of the blood-brain barrier in protecting against environmental toxins and pathogens. He emphasizes that many people suffer from chronic health issues due to the exposure to these toxins, which the body is ill-equipped to handle. The conversation highlights the need for greater awareness and understanding of how industrial chemicals affect our health, challenging mainstream medical narratives.
