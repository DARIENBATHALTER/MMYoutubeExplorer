In this episode of the Medical Medium Radio Show, Anthony William discusses the importance of clean, untampered health information and highlights the issues with agenda-driven health research. Emphasizing the significance of choosing the right supplements and foods during pregnancy, he critiques conventional prenatal vitamins and stresses the value of whole foods and specific nutrients like spirulina and barley grass juice powder. He also addresses the misconceptions about protein needs during pregnancy and advocates for a balanced diet that supports both mother and baby, warning against trendy health traps and the overuse of vitamin D.
