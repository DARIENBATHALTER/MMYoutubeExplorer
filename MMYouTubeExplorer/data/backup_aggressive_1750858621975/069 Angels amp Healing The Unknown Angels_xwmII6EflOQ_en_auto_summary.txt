In this episode of the Medical Medium podcast, Anthony Williams discusses the prevalence of health struggles among individuals, emphasizing that many suffer from conditions like anxiety and autoimmune disorders. He introduces the concept of angels, specifically the 'unknown angels,' who are believed to assist people in their times of need and are often overlooked compared to the more popular archangels. The discussion highlights the importance of faith in these angels, their distinctive characteristics, and how to effectively reach out to them for help.
