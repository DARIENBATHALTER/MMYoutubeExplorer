In this episode of the Medical Medium podcast, Anthony Williams discusses the misconceptions surrounding parasites and their alleged role in chronic illnesses. He emphasizes that while parasites do exist, many people mistakenly attribute their health issues to them rather than understanding the true underlying causes, such as stealth viruses and other pathogens. Williams encourages listeners to focus on accurate information and effective healing protocols instead of getting caught up in the parasitic health trends that have not proven effective.
