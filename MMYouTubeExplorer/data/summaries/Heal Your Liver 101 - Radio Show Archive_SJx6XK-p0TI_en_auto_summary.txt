In this episode of the Medical Medium Radio Show, Anthony William discusses the importance of liver health and the factors contributing to chronic illnesses, emphasizing that science and conventional medicine have not fully resolved these issues. He highlights the significance of detoxifying the liver from various toxins found in everyday environments and recommends specific foods, herbs, and supplements to promote liver health. Additionally, William critiques the widespread focus on protein consumption and encourages listeners to lower fat intake to better support liver function and overall health.
