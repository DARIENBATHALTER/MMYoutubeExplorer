The video discusses the widespread consumption of nutritional yeast and its potential health implications, emphasizing that it is often made from GMO sugar beets, which can alter the yeast's cell structure, making it a concern for health. The speaker highlights that nutritional yeast is marketed for its nutrient content but argues that it doesn't provide significant health benefits compared to natural foods. Concerns are also raised about the presence of glutamic acid in nutritional yeast, which can mimic MSG, leading to potential addiction and adverse effects on brain and gut health.
