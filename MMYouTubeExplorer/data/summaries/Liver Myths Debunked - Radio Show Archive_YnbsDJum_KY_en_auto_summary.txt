In this episode, Anthony William, the Medical Medium, discusses common misconceptions about liver health, including the myths surrounding liver flushing, liver stones, and the consumption of animal liver. He emphasizes that eating liver can be harmful due to its toxic nature and that practices like taking ox bile and liver flushing can do more harm than good. Instead, he advocates for using celery juice to support liver health and encourages viewers to educate themselves through his book, Liver Rescue.
