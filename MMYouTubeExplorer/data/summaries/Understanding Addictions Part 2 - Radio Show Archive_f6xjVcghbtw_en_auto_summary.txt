In this episode of the Medical Medium radio show, Anthony William continues his discussion on addiction, emphasizing the importance of understanding the underlying causes and mechanisms of various addictions. He explores the roles of adrenaline and glucose in addiction, providing insights into how awareness of these factors can help individuals manage and overcome their addictive behaviors. William also shares personal anecdotes and practical advice, including the potential benefits of certain foods like chocolate for those struggling with addiction.
