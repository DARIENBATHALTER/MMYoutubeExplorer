In this episode, Anthony William discusses the complexities of chronic illness and the misinformation surrounding autoimmune diseases, emphasizing that the body does not attack itself, but rather responds to pathogens. He highlights the role of grains in health, particularly focusing on how certain grains can feed pathogens that exacerbate health conditions. William encourages listeners to rethink their diet, particularly with grains, and to explore healthier options while spreading awareness about accurate health information.
