In this episode, Anthony Williams discusses the prevalence of natural flavors in food products and their hidden risks, particularly focusing on monosodium glutamate (MSG) and its effects on health. He highlights the deceptive nature of labeling, where natural flavors can contain harmful additives that consumers are often unaware of. The podcast emphasizes the importance of transparency in food labeling and raises awareness about the potential health implications of consuming these chemical flavorings.
