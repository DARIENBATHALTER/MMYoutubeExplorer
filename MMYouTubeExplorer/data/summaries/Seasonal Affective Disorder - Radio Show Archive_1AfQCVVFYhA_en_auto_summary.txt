In this episode of the Medical Medium radio show, Anthony William discusses Seasonal Affective Disorder (SAD), arguing that it is often misdiagnosed and that the symptoms attributed to SAD can be linked to more complex underlying health issues. He emphasizes the importance of addressing nutritional deficiencies and supporting the central nervous system through supplements like B12 and zinc, as well as incorporating nutrient-rich foods such as spirulina and fruits. Anthony encourages listeners to explore holistic approaches to improve their health and not rely solely on labels like SAD, advocating for a deeper understanding of personal health conditions and proactive healing strategies.
