In this episode of the Medical Medium radio show, Anthony William discusses urinary tract infections (UTIs), emphasizing the importance of understanding their underlying causes, particularly the role of strep bacteria. He shares his experiences helping people find relief from UTIs without antibiotics, focusing on dietary changes and the use of fruits like cranberries and elderberries to combat infections. The episode highlights the need for a clean and informed approach to health, free from the influence of interest groups and faulty research.
