In this episode, Anthony William discusses Vitiligo, emphasizing the importance of understanding its causes and the misinformation surrounding autoimmune diagnoses in the medical community. He explains how industrial chemicals, particularly aluminum, and dietary fats contribute to the condition, and provides dietary recommendations to help manage and potentially reverse Vitiligo. The focus is on lowering fat intake, removing dairy and gluten, and incorporating specific foods like celery juice and heavy metal detox smoothies to aid healing.
