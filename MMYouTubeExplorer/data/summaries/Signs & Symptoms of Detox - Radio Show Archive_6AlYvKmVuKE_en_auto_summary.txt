In this episode of the Medical Medium Radio Show, Anthony William discusses the importance of detoxing and cleansing, emphasizing that many available programs and products can be misleading and potentially harmful. He advises listeners to gradually lower fat intake and switch to a clean diet before attempting more radical detoxes to avoid shocking the body and compromising the immune system. William also stresses the significance of understanding one's own body and symptoms during detoxification to effectively navigate the process without relying on expensive cleansing products that may not be beneficial.
