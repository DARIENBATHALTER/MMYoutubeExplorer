In this episode, Anthony William discusses the hidden dangers of consuming eggs and their connection to various health issues. He explains how the medical industry has used eggs in laboratories to sustain and breed pathogens, contributing to chronic illnesses. The podcast aims to raise awareness about the impact of eggs on health and encourages listeners to reconsider their dietary choices.
