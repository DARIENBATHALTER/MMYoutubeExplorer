In this episode of the Medical Medium radio show, Anthony William discusses the complexities of eating disorders, emphasizing that they affect everyone in some way. He highlights the historical context of dietary guidelines and their impact on health, particularly focusing on how heavy metal toxicity, especially mercury, plays a significant role in conditions like bulimia and anorexia. William insists on the importance of understanding these disorders beyond conventional views, advocating for a holistic approach to healing through proper nutrition and addressing underlying health issues.
