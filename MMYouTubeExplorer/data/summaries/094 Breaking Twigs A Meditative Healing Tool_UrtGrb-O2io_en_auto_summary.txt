In this episode of the Medical Medium Podcast, Anthony William introduces a unique spiritual healing practice involving breaking small twigs as a form of meditation and therapy. This technique, rooted in ancient practices, is said to rewire the consciousness and soul, providing mental clarity and emotional relief by utilizing the natural sound of snapping twigs. The exercise offers benefits such as reducing stress, breaking emotional triggers, and enhancing communication, both individually and in group settings.
