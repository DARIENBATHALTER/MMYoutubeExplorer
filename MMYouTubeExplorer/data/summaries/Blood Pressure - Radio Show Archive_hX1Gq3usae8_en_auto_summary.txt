In this episode, Anthony William discusses the mysteries surrounding blood pressure issues, emphasizing that many people experience either high or low blood pressure without any obvious cardiovascular disease. He attributes these conditions to liver health and the impact of diet, encouraging listeners to consider the effects of processed foods and fats on their blood pressure. William provides insights on how to manage and improve blood pressure through dietary changes and natural remedies.
