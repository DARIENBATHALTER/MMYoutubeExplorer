In this episode of the Medical Medium podcast, Anthony Williams discusses the concept of 'chumming' and how it relates to chronic illness. He explains that many people unknowingly feed pathogens and viruses within their bodies through their daily diets, leading to various health issues. The conversation emphasizes the importance of understanding these processes to reclaim health and avoid feeding the very conditions that make people sick.
