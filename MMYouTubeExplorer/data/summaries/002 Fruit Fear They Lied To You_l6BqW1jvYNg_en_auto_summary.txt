In this episode, Anthony William discusses the widespread fear surrounding fruit, which he argues is a harmful narrative perpetuated by certain health authorities. He highlights the essential role of fruit in maintaining health and the potential ecological disaster if fruit consumption continues to decline. William emphasizes that misinformation about fruit could exacerbate health issues, including infertility and chronic illnesses, while also damaging the environment and food supply.
